Good News

The Gospel

The most important news anyone can hear is that God has made a way for sinners to be saved. Here it is, step by step, straight from the Bible.

God

Who He Is

God is holy, perfect, and the rightful Creator of all things. Because He is perfect, His standard is perfection, and He cannot simply overlook sin.

He made you on purpose, and He loves you. But because He is perfectly holy and just, He cannot pretend that wrong is not wrong. The same God who gives us life is the God we will each one day answer to, and He has every right to set the standard for how we should live.

Genesis 1:1 Matthew 5:48

The Problem

Sin

Every one of us has sinned and fallen short of God's glory. Our sin separates us from a holy God.

Sin is more than the obvious things we tend to notice in other people. It is every time we have lied, hated, taken what was not ours, or simply lived for ourselves instead of for God. None of us measures up to His perfect standard, and deep down we know it. That sin builds a wall between us and the God who made us.

Romans 3:23 Isaiah 59:2

The Penalty

Death

The just penalty for sin is death and eternal separation from God. No amount of good works can pay a debt this large.

The Bible describes this penalty as a real and eternal separation from God. Because God is perfectly just, He will not leave sin unpunished, any more than a good judge would let a guilty criminal simply walk free. Trying harder, turning over a new leaf, or hoping our good outweighs our bad can never erase a debt we have already run up.

Romans 6:23 Hebrews 9:27

The Payment

What Christ Did

Jesus Christ lived a sinless life, died on the cross in our place, and rose again the third day. He paid the debt we could never pay.

On the cross, Jesus took the exact punishment we deserved, dying as our substitute so that God's justice was fully satisfied. Three days later He rose bodily from the grave, proving He had conquered sin and death. This is the heart of the good news: the price has already been paid in full, and it was paid by Someone who loves you.

Romans 5:8 John 3:16

The Decision

Faith

God calls you to turn from sin and trust Christ alone to save you. Not church membership, not good works, but faith in Him.

This means turning from going your own way and placing your full trust in what Jesus did for you, the way you would trust a bridge to hold your weight. Salvation is a free gift; you cannot earn it, you can only receive it. Anyone who sincerely calls on the Lord Jesus to save them, He will save, and He promises to keep them forever.

Acts 16:31 Romans 10:13

Would You Like To Know More?

If God is speaking to your heart, we would count it a privilege to sit down with you, open the Bible, and answer any question you have. There is no pressure, just a willing friend.
